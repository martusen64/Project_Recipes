Good ol' grandma's christmas cannellonis

Description

This is the typical catalonian christmas dish made by "abuelas" all around Catalonia.

Simply by imagining the tasteiest most exquisit meat paste, rolled into a pasta blunt

and glazed by a delicious bechamel you can start to imagine the absolute banger that this is.

Ingredients

  • Beef meat
  • Pig meat
  • Chicken breast
  • Foiegras
  • Onion
  • Bechamel(400ml(americans cry abt it))
  • Them Pasta blunts
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Tomato
  • Brandy yk
