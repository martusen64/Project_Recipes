Good ol' grandma's christmas cannellonis

Description

This is the typical catalonian christmas dish made by "abuelas" all around Catalonia.

Simply by imagining the tasteiest most exquisit meat paste, rolled into a pasta blunt

and glazed by a delicious bechamel you can start to imagine the absolute banger that this is.

Ingredients

  • Beef meat
  • Pig meat
  • Chicken breast
  • Foiegras
  • Onion
  • Bechamel(400ml(americans cry abt it))
  • Them Pasta blunts
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Tomato
  • Brandy yk

Steps

  1. Blend all the beef
  2. Cook the pasta
  3. Cook the bechamel (if you don't have it already prepared)
  4. Roll the pasta blunts filled with the beef paste
  5. Pour the bechamel over the cannellonis
  6. Put some cheese over the cannellonis
  7. Bake it all
  8. Enjoy
