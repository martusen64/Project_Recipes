Quick and nourishing cuban rice

Description

This is a classic recipe for a quick and nourishing rice which is also delicious.

Ingredients

  • Two eggs
  • Garlic
  • Rice
  • Fried tomato sauce

Steps

  1. Cook the rice with a garlic clove
  2. Once the rice is cooked, fry both eggs (don't cook the yoke pls)
  3. Mix the fried tomato sauce with the rice and the eggs
  4. Enjoy
