Delicious and healthy pasta sauce

Description

This is an exquisit pasta sauce which includes protein and vegetables with some tomato.

Ingredients

  • Beef meat
  • Onion
  • Zucchini
  • Pepper
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Tomato Sauce (real tomato pls)

Steps

  1. Stir-fry the onion
  2. Once the onion is nearly fried, stir-fry the zucchini
  3. Same with the pepper
  4. On the side stir-fry the beef meat
  5. Once everything is fried, mix it with the tomato sauce
  6. Cook your pasta/li>
  7. Mix it (with some of the cooking water from the pasta)
  8. Enjoy
